What are the current trends in sustainable residential development?

Current trends in sustainable residential development include:

  • **Net-Zero Energy Homes:** Designing homes that produce as much energy as they consume, often through solar panels and advanced insulation.
  • **Water Conservation Systems:** Implementing rainwater harvesting, greywater recycling, and efficient fixtures to reduce water usage.
  • **Use of Eco-Friendly Materials:** Prioritizing recycled, locally sourced, and low-impact building materials.
  • **Smart Home Technology:** Integrating systems for optimized energy use, lighting, and climate control.
  • **Biophilic Design:** Incorporating natural elements and green spaces to enhance occupant well-being and connection to nature.
